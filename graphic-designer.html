Graphic Designer

Overview

In this role, I was primarily responsible for shaping the visual identity of our application, defining key elements such as the logo, fonts, and colour scheme. I was also responsible for making promotional videos and posters that adheres with our branding.

Technical Challenges

  • Making designs that will consistently adhere to our determined branding and colour scheme, while also being visually appealing.
Moodboard

To kick off this creative process, I initiated the development of a moodboard, curating a collection of compelling graphics and visuals sourced from Pinterest. This moodboard served as a rich source of inspiration for our team, offering a visual direction for the overall look and feel of our application before delving into the specifics of logo and prototype development.

Logo Design

I designed several logo concepts using Adobe Photoshop based on the colour scheme and some assets I created. We would like a clean and modern look for our application, which is why we decided to use a sans serif font. We also liked both the bar and line graph and the blue colour for the text as it provides more appeal and colour to it, so we ended up choosing this final logo through a vote:

Promotional Video

For the Expo, we had to make a two minutes promotional video. I decided to take on this responsibility as it is related to my specialism as the graphic designer. I also had some prior experiences of video editing for several events and projects previously so I know that I can produce a video that would showcase our app in a nice and professional way. I took the role as the Producer, Director and Editor of the video. I organised video shootings with Florian, a third year Film and TV Production student at DkIT who helped us take professional video shoots as a Videographer. After that, I also recorded some demos of the app and edited it into animated mockups. I made the mockups using Adobe Photoshop and Previewed, and edited the video using Adobe Premiere Pro.

Poster & Leaflet

For the Expo, we wanted to have printed posters that would catch the visitor's attention and give an overview of our app. I designed the poster using Adobe Photoshop.

In addition to that, I also designed a leaflet which showcases our app's key functionalities to be handed to the visitors in our Expo.

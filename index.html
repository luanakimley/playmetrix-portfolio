Unified Team Management: Tailoring PlayMetrix for Every User Type

In my individual portfolio, I aim to showcase how I'm developing PlayMetrix to really connect with its users and their roles within a GAA team. Our app caters to various team positions, so it's vital that each role has a smooth onboarding process and a pleasant experience while using the app. I focus on making sure the interface is easy to understand and navigate, so everyone on the team can jump right in without any hassle.

By putting user needs first, I'm working to create an app that feels like a natural fit for every member of the GAA team. To achieve this I will be doing a usability overview for several different tasks for the users across roles, detailing potential/existing problems, applied solutions, and/or proposed solutions for future enhancements through design and/or technical perspectives.
